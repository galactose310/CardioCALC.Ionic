Le score HEMORR2HAGES permet d'estimer le risque hémorragique d'un patient en fibrillation atriale sous traitement anticoagulant par Warfarine.

Source : Clinical classification schemes for predicting hemorrhage: results from the National Registry of Atrial Fibrillation (NRAF). Gage BF, et al. Am Heart Journal, 2006;151(3):713-9.

Validation : Performance of the HEMORR2HAGES, ATRIA, and HAS-BLED bleeding risk–prediction scores in patients with atrial fibrillation undergoing anticoagulation. Apostolakis S, et al. J Am Coll Cardiol, 2012;60(9):861-7.
