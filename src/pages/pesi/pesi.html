Le score PESI (Pulmonary Embolism Severity Index) évalue la gravité clinique d'une embolie pulmonaire aiguë, en-dehors des situations d'état de choc ou d'hypotension artérielle sévère.

Source : Derivation and validation of a prognostic model for pulmonary embolism. Aujesky D, et al. Am J Respir Crit Care Med, 2005;172(8):1041-6.

Validation : Prospective validation of the Pulmonary Embolism Severity Index. A clinical prognostic model for pulmonary embolism. Donzé J, et al. Thrombosis and Haemostasis, 2008;100(5):943-8.

Recommandations : 2014 ESC Guidelines on the diagnosis and management of acute pulmonary embolism. Konstantinides SV, et al. European Heart Journal, 2014;35(43):3033-69, 3069a-3069k.
